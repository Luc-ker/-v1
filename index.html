砌字遊戲 (Chinese Word Puzzle)
等級 (Level): 1
時間 (Time): 3:00

分數 (Score): 0
遊戲規則:
• 選擇兩個漢字來創建一個新詞
• 如果正確，您將獲得一分
• 有 5 個級別 - 要進入下一個級別，您分別需要 5、10、15 和 20 點
• 第 5 級是『無盡模式』，您將嘗試在剩餘時間內獲得盡可能多的積分
Rules:
• Select two Chinese characters to create a new word
• If correct, you gain a point
• There are 5 levels - to progress to the next level you need 5, 10, 15 and 20 points respectively
• Level 5 is an "Endless Mode" where you will try and obtain as many points as possible within your remaining time
